About This Project

This project explores the dynamics of customer retention within the financial industry. By leveraging a binary classification model, it predicts customer churn and provides valuable insights into customer loyalty and relationship management. This analysis empowers organizations to address challenges in maintaining customer engagement effectively.

How It Works

The application allows users to input customer details such as age, geography, gender, number of products, balance, and whether the customer is active or not. It utilizes a trained machine learning model to predict whether the customer is likely to churn or remain with the bank. The prediction result is displayed, helping stakeholders make informed decisions.
